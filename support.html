Volunteer roles

Find where you can contribute.

Each volunteer role is designed to be specific. You do not need to do everything. You only need to bring your strengths to the part of Digital Disha where they fit best.

Role 01

Teaching Volunteers

Teaching volunteers help students during live sessions and hands-on practice.

What you would do

Assist during computer basics and digital literacy sessions
Help students practice tools like Word, PowerPoint, Canva, internet, Gmail, and HTML
Support students one-on-one during activities and projects

Best for

Students, peer educators, and anyone comfortable explaining basic digital tools with patience.

Apply through Volunteer Form
Role 02

Curriculum & Content Volunteers

Curriculum volunteers help shape what Digital Disha teaches and how it is taught.

What you would do

Create lesson plans, worksheets, and practice tasks
Design beginner-friendly activities and mini projects
Update content for AI awareness, online safety, and newer digital tools

Best for

People who enjoy writing, organizing information, teaching material, or educational design.

Apply through Volunteer Form
Role 03

Design & Media Volunteers

Design and media volunteers make Digital Disha’s learning material and online presence more engaging.

What you would do

Create Canva slides, posters, certificates, and worksheets
Edit photos, videos, reels, or cohort visuals
Help organize gallery content and maintain a clean visual identity

Best for

People interested in Canva, design, photography, video editing, social media, or storytelling.

Apply through Volunteer Form
Role 04

Outreach & Partnerships Volunteers

Outreach volunteers help Digital Disha reach the next classroom, school, or partner.

What you would do

Identify schools, orphanages, community centers, or youth spaces
Help communicate with teachers, principals, organizations, or sponsors
Draft outreach emails and maintain follow-up records

Best for

Strong communicators, organizers, email writers, researchers, or people who enjoy coordination.

Apply through Volunteer Form
